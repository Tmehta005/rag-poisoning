STANCE: {{"answer": "<your current best answer as a short sentence>", "confidence": <float in [0.0, 1.0]>, "citations": ["<doc_id>", ...]}}

Before the STANCE line you may include a short argument (at most ~6 sentences) explaining your position and, in later rounds, how it responds to the other agents. Do not invent citations; cite only doc_ids returned by retrieve.
